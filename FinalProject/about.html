About The Game

Welcome to Tarheel Tic-Tac-Toe! This game allows users of all abilities to play with an artificially intelligent robot. We have created a Tic-Tac-Toe game at three different levels: Grasshopper, Novice, and Sensei. If you believe that you have not yet fully trained to battle our very smart Sensei, feel free to begin as a young Newbie. Once you have trained, try to beat the Skilled AI. Don't move forward until you have trained enough to beat the evil Skilled robot. Sensei AI is too powerful and not enough practice could make the game very difficult. However, if you feel that you are ready to take on the challenge and battle Sensei AI, he is ready and waiting!
